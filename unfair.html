Unfairness of Golf Handicaps for Betting

This paper by Ian G McHale analyzed the probability of winning a head-to-head game when the handicap of the two players was different.

If the golf handicapping system was fair, the four lines would be identical, horizontal and intersect the y axis at 0.5.
